# 🏥 NextStep-Care: Bridging the Gap in Post-Hospital Recovery

**NextStep-Care** is an AI-driven, full-stack healthcare ecosystem designed to eliminate the "Transition Gap"—the dangerous window after a patient is discharged from the hospital. 

By integrating **Google Gemini AI** and a dual-interface management system, we ensure that recovery doesn't stop at the hospital exit.

## 🚩 The Problem: The "Silent Crisis" of Readmissions
Every year, millions of patients are discharged with complex recovery plans, but the system is fundamentally broken:
* **The 20% Trap:** Approximately **20% of patients** are readmitted within 30 days due to a lack of immediate follow-up.
* **Preventability:** Research shows that **27% of these readmissions are potentially preventable** through better communication.
* **The Information Void:** Only **12-34% of discharge summaries** reach follow-up providers in time for the first post-hospital visit.

---

## 🔍 The "Recovery Gap" We Found
1. **The Triage Gap:** Patients experience symptoms at 2:00 AM but have no way to know if they need an ER or just rest.
2. **The Compliance Gap:** Medication adherence drops significantly when patients feel "on their own" post-discharge.
3. **The Data Gap:** Doctors lose visibility into patient vitals the moment the patient leaves the ward.

---

## 💡 Our Solution: NextStep-Care
We didn't just build a dashboard; we built a **Continuous Care Bridge**.

### **🚀 Key Product Features**

#### **🤖 ASI:One AI Triage Engine**
* **Context-Aware Guidance:** Powered by **Google Gemini**, the AI analyzes specific conditions to provide personalized recovery advice.
* **Red-Flag Detection:** Automatically recognizes "danger signs" and instructs patients to seek immediate medical help.
* **24/7 Availability:** Bridges the gap when doctors are unavailable, reducing patient anxiety.

#### **🩺 Physician Command Center**
* **Unified Patient Oversight:** Doctors monitor an entire ward of discharged patients from a single view.
* **Smart Vitals Monitoring:** Real-time tracking of BP, Blood Sugar, and Hemoglobin with color-coded "Critical Status" alerts.
* **Digital Prescriptions:** issue and update medication plans that sync instantly to the patient’s dashboard.

#### **📱 Patient Recovery Hub**
* **Medication Adherence Tracker:** Interactive checklist for daily prescriptions to ensure zero missed doses.
* **Vitals Logging & Trends:** Simple interface for patients to record metrics, visualized via dynamic recovery graphs.
* **Emergency SOS:** A one-click trigger simulating an immediate alert to the care team.

#### **📊 Real-Time Health Analytics**
* **Dynamic Visualization:** Integration of `Chart.js` to transform raw medical numbers into easy-to-read recovery trends for both doctors and patients.

* **Intelligence:** Google Gemini API (NLP for medical triage)
